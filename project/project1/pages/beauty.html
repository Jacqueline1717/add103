Eye Candy

Pictured below is not just a flower, not just my nails, and not just the sky.

I love photography, and I noticed there were raindrops on the petals, leaves, and stem. The flower was a similar pale white to the clouds—remnants of a beautiful storm— and I had to take a photo. I was able to capture a million beautiful things in one picture.

I began doing my own gel nails in 2020 and have consistently done them since. Over time, I’ve explored a variety of designs, colors, shapes, and concepts. Nail art has become a personal form of expression and a skill I’ve refined through practice and creativity.

I’ve always been intrigued by storms—the dark sky, flashes of lightning, and thunder that gives you goosebumps. My mom and I often sit outside and record lightning storms. The sky becomes a gallery of beauty and mystery; in this picture you can almost see a figure holding a weapon in the clouds.
